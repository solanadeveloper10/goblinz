Who are we?

Frenquently Asked Questions

What data do you own about your players?

If you suscribed to our newsletter, we know your e-mail address, and maybe your name (or possibly surname or nickname).

If you used our in-game feedback system, we only collect your textual feedback. No other data are collected and feedback are anonymous (we are unable to know who sent the feedback) if you don’t write your email.

Some of our games collect anonymous data about bugs and crashes. There is no personal data collected, not even your IP address without your consent. Only the crash logs are sent to us.

Where and why are my information used?

These information are used to send you our newsletter, and occasionnal news about our games (only if you subscribed).

We do not use them to profile or extract habits.

Feedback from our in-game feedback system help us to improve our games.

Who can access my information?

Nobody but us. None of your data are public.

We do not share your data with third parties.

We do not resell your data.

How long do you keep my data?

As long as you are subscribed to our newsletter. We do not keep your data if you unsubscribe.
